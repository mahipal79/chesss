About Us

Welcome to our chess website! We are a group of enthusiastic students who have developed this website as our web technology project under the guidance of Professor Ms. Mosam Patel. Our aim is to provide chess enthusiasts with a platform to learn, practice, and enjoy the game of chess.

We would also like to thank our fellow classmates for their contributions and collaboration during this project. Their dedication and teamwork have made this endeavor a success.
